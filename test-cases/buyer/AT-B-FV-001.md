# AT-B-FV-001: Add Item to Cart

**Feature:** @cart
**Role:** @buyer
**Priority:** @regression

## Precondition

- Already on explore page
- At least one product visible

## Steps

1. Click first product in explore grid
2. Click "Add to Cart" button
3. Go to cart page

## Expected

- Cart page shows the added product
- Cart item count shows 1
- Price matches the product price

## Test Data

- product: first visible product on explore page
